Flyers vs Posters

Flyers are compact and ideal for handouts or online promotion, while posters are larger and perfect for events or announcements with high visibility needs.

Banners vs Social Graphics

Banners are great for headers and ads (print or digital), whereas social graphics are tailored to fit Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn formats for daily brand interaction.
